# Venue Research — Portland, Oregon

## Research in Progress

We're researching suitable venues in Portland, OR for security workshops and monthly meetups.

### Requirements
- Meeting room for 20-50 people
- WiFi/internet access
- Projector/screen capability
- Wheelchair accessibility
- Reasonable cost or free for community groups
- Evening availability

### Venue Categories Being Explored
- Community centers
- Coworking spaces
- Conference/meeting venues
- Library meeting rooms
- University spaces
